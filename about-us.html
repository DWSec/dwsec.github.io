Our journey

Through the Years

CA 1992

Access to Board Bulletin Systems & first modems

X25 Networks and war dialling was among the first things we started to investigate along with some computer viruses. Oh well the old good days!

CA 1994

Security and Vulnerability Research

The year we started to drive our attention around Unix Systems and Internet Protocols along with memory corruption vulnerabilities.

CA 2000

Windows 2000 Platform and the growing Internet

Security Industry as we know it nowadays, with plenty of Training, Infrastructure Pen Testing Assessments and Hardening of various OS.

CA 2004

Web Application Assessments started to grow exponentially

Working with one of the best teams in the world (NGS) we were able to capitalise this demand for today's market.

CA 2010

Our own journey and businesses in the UK

Our own businesses took form becoming independent boutique security companies and home of the most talented people around the security industry.

CA 2019

Initial launch of Dark Waves Infosec LTD

Dark Waves Infosec LTD was born joining forces with the most talented people around the industry and strengthening our Research & Development branch.
